Cast Iron Oven Baked Pizza

Description

Indulge in the mouthwatering delight of a crispy cast iron pepperoni pizza. Picture a perfect golden crust, crackling under your teeth with a blend of crispy and chewy textures. The savory pepperoni curls crisp up in the oven, releasing their smoky essence. The melted mozzarella forms a creamy, gooey layer, while the tangy tomato sauce, infused with herbs and spices, ties everything together. This masterpiece combines rustic charm with classic flavors, creating an irresistible symphony of textures and tastes that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Instant yeast
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Olive oil
Toppings
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Measure out ingredients
  2. Put in mixer for 7 minutes
  3. Let pizza dough proof for 2 hours at room temperature covered
  4. Roll dough out to 1cm thickness
  5. Oil the cast iron pan
  6. Place pizza dough in cast iron
  7. Top pizza
  8. Cook pizza at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown
